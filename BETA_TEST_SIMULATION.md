# Subtaste Beta Test Simulation

> 40 simulated testers using **real companies and real people** — to identify the actual market.
> Each rates out of 10 and provides feedback. This is how we find our first customers.

**Why real names?** Fictional personas tell you what you already believe. Real people force you to think about actual buying decisions, real org charts, and specific reasons someone would or wouldn't pay. If you can't imagine the real Gustav Söderström at Spotify caring, you haven't built something worth pitching.
